#  SheLeads AI

### From Idea to Income in Days, Not Months

AI-powered micro-business assistant helping women entrepreneurs grow sustainable businesses through smart pricing, demand insights, and voice-based mentorship.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 📸 AI Product Analysis
- 📈 Local Demand Insights
- 🎤 Voice-Based Business Mentor
- 📝 AI Marketing Caption Generator
- 🌍 Multilingual Accessibility
- 📊 Smart Business Recommendations

---

## 🌟 Problem Statement

Millions of women in Tier 2, Tier 3, and rural communities possess valuable skills such as tailoring, food preparation, handicrafts, and handmade product creation, but lack access to:

- Business guidance
- Pricing knowledge
- Marketing support
- Digital tools
- Market demand insights

This prevents many women from converting their skills into sustainable income opportunities.

---

## 💡 Solution

SheLeads AI is an AI-powered entrepreneurship assistant designed to help women confidently start and grow micro-businesses.

The platform provides:

- Smart pricing suggestions
- AI-generated marketing captions
- Local demand insights
- Voice-based mentorship
- Beginner-friendly dashboard experience

Built with accessibility and simplicity in mind, SheLeads AI helps women grow sustainable businesses without requiring prior business knowledge.

---

# 🧠 AI Workflow

1. User uploads product image or voice query
2. Frontend sends request to backend API
3. Gemini AI processes business insights
4. Personalized recommendations are generated
5. Data stored in MongoDB
6. Dashboard displays results
